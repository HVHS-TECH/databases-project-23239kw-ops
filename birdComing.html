BIRD COMING

Bird coming is a rare game about birds. You, the player, have the sole responsiblity of protecting the city. Long long long ago, 2016 in Northern Pakistan a cave was unearthed by a team of scientists. In the cave was a vial filled with the most potent hallucinagen known to man. Unfortunately one of the scientists let the glass vial slip from his grip, the liquid inside seeped into the ground, into the water, and soon into the vegetation. Unknown to everyone at the time this poison only affected egg-bearing species. This includes: all avian species and turtles. The birds picked contaminated grass to construct their nests and thus infected themselves with the hallucinagen. Birds around the globe quickly went crazy and humanity was brought to its knees. Now in the present day, you, the player, are the sole protector of the final bastion of the human race - Chicago. The birds are coming and your cannon is steaming. Dont let the birds come.
