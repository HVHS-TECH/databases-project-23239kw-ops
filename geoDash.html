GAME 2

Game 2 is an even rarer game about geometry. Ancient Rome was a place known for its healthcare. A famous Roman doctor by the name of Giovanni Dash was among the brightest in this field. One day, during a royal parade attended by the emperor he had the honour to meet with Julius Caesar himself. Shaking his hand,Giovanni noticed the emperor had a necrotic lesion on his lower neck area. Immediately he warned the emperor but Caesar would not hear it. Giovanni was imprisoned and forced to partake in a colloseum game. The day of the game arrived and Giovanni walked out to a raring crowd. Before him were massive spikes that shot at him with break neck speed and he had to jump to avoid them. This would go on to inspire this very game, this game allows players to relive Giovanni's last moments in the colosseum. It was originally named: "spike jumper" but was later renamed as: "Geo Dash", In honour of Giovanni Dash.
